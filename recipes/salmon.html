Salmon

This simple salmon recipe is for salmon with white rice. An addtional option is adding your favorite vegetable dish, but not required.

White rice is recommended to be cooked using a rice cooker for ease.

The salmon is recommended to be seasoned after the oven has preheated and before placing it into the oven. This is a simple quick and easy dish for days cooking doesn't feel ideal but needs to be done.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Jasmine white rice
  • 2lb Atlantic Salmon
  • 4tbsp of Lemon Pepper OR Cajun OR Garlic Salt

Steps

  1. Add white rice to rice cooker pot, rinse until water runs close to clear, and begin cooking
  2. Preheate oven to 375 degrees. Either wait to begin preheating for hotter salmon, or begin after starting rice to allow salmon to cool down
  3. Add foil to baking tray and then the salmon
  4. Season the salmon, top side if skin remains on bottom, both sides if there is no skin
  5. Add salmon to preheated oven
  6. Turn off the oven and remove the salmon from the oven after 17 minutes
  7. Serve with white rice when finished
